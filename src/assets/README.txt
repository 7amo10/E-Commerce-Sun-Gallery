Place the brand logo here as 'handmade-logo.webp' (or .png).
Recommended sizes:
- favicon/social: 1200x630 (og:image)
- header/logo: 400x400 (rounded)

You can download the logo from:
https://cdn.builder.io/api/v1/image/assets%2F561f03c0a72e445aaba6ba92ed330a22%2Fb278b93baa6a483ebff28ef2e34bce70?format=webp&width=800

After placing the file, the header will use '/assets/handmade-logo.webp' automatically and the favicon meta will be used by browsers.
